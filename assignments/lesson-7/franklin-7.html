Franklin Breaks Record

Record winds hit Franklin this past weekend taking residents by surprise. It's uncommon to have winds of this severity, especially at this time of year. Residents are joining forces to clean up the streets. Record winds downed trees, power lines, and littered the community with debris. As you can see, Mother Nature has decided to do a 180-degree turn bringing warmer temperatures our way for the Ten Day Forecast. For those participating in community clean-up efforts, we advise you to stay well-hydrated as you work outside for the next 2 days. While not extreme temps, the change from 65 degrees to a sudden 89 might take some acclimating. Be careful out there!
