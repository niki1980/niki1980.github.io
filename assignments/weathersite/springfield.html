Springfield is Glowing

It's a beautiful time of year to live in or visit Springfield! Temperatures at sunrise are perfect for working in the fields, taking a leisurely morning stroll, or going for a bike ride. Warmer, yet comfortable afternoon temps mean harvest time is right around the corner. The town council is busy preparing for the 16th Annual Springfield Harvest Festival which is sure to draw visitors from miles around. There will be food, fun crafts for the kids, games, and more. There's no greater place to be than Springfield at this time of year. Get out there and enjoy time with your community and this beautiful weather!
