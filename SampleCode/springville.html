Elegant Sunrise Graces This Year's Tulip Festival

This year's mild and rainy spring weather has created the ideal growing conditions for tulips, which should produce a lovely flower display at the Springfield Tulip Festival. The festival will run from April 7 - May 1 and will include free tours of Springfield's lovely tulip fields and gardens, as well as an opportunity to purchase bulbs directly from our local growers. Bella Gardens, Springfield's premiere tulip growers, will be presenting their newly developed tulip variety called, Elegant Sunrise. This event is sure to be the highlight of the tulip festival this year. Amber Lee, of the Tri-City Horticulture Society, mentioned that Elegant Sunrise is among the most anticipated tulip varieties within the United States. It is her opinion that Bella Gardens' tulip program is one of the finest in the country, and that Elegant Sunrise is poised to compete for a top national award. To find out more about Elegant Sunrise or other award winning tulip varieties, please visit the Bella Gardens website. For more information about the tulip festival, visit the Springfield Tulip Festival page on the Springfield city website.
