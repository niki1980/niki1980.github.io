Fun in the Summer Sun

Are you ready for some fun in the sun this summer? Weather predictions for the Tri-City area are expected to be slightly warmer and somewhat sunnier that usual this summer. We've been out scouting around for some fun summer activities for families to enjoy together right here in Greenville. One idea is to take a ferry ride across Green Water Bay. Occasionally there are sightings of sea lions, porpoises, or even Orca's from the ferry. There's also a great old-fashioned ice cream shop near the ferry terminal, which serves 17 varieties of ice cream, including huckleberry, which is a local favorite. If you enjoy fishing or crabbing, the Mooreland fishing peer, which is just three blocks from the ferry terminal, is sponsoring two free fishing days this summer for children under twelve. This means they will not be required to obtain a license. However, there is a limit of two fish and one crab per child. For more information and ideas about fun outdoor activities in the Greenville area, please visit the City of Greenville website.
